  • Produce two charts that support or refute (or are related to in some way) a topic discussed at the Festival of Economics. https://www.economicsobservatory.com/festival-of-economics-2024

  • Set out (not more than 25 words) a topic that was discussed. • Make two charts that support or refute it or a related to an argument on this topic. This could be two that support, two that refute, or a mixture. • Comment (not more than 25 words) on what you find. Your commentary should be placed near the chart in a way that makes it intuitive to your site’s users (i.e., above, below, or to the side).
