Coding Challenge 3 – Using a visualisation in economic/policy commentary

The kids aren’t alright: Tackling a legacy of child poverty, bad diets and poor health (Link).

In this event, the expert panel discussed issues affecting young children in the UK, such as poverty and health, and how they might affect health outcomes.

The UK recently witnessed an increase in the percentage of children living in low-income households, which is alarming when looking at the long-term trend. Both measures have almost always followed a downward trend since they were introduced, even during the COVID-19 pandemic.

Child obeseity on the other hand has recoevered to almost pre-COVID levels among 10-11 year-olds, and has gone down even further among 4-5 year-olds.
