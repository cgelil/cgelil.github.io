Drivers of Food Insecurity: Case Study on MENA

Project Overview

This project explores the trends and drivers behind food insecurity across the Middle East and North Africa (MENA) region. We utilize IPC Phase 3+ data to track populations experiencing severe food insecurity between 2017 and 2023.
